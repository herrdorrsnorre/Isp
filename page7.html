Music

The gaohu is the most important instrument in this music and almost always leads the melody with the other instruments as a support. Most music is played is 2/4 or 4/4 and is either played at presto(very fast) or adagio(very slow). It usually has an upbeat tone and uses pentatonic scales (5 notes per octave) instead of heptatonic scales (7 notes and what we use)

C major pentatonic scale

In the culture of hong kong music is used everywhere it can used as entertainment, a way to relax and on special occasions such as Chinese new year and the dragon boat festival or for the dragon dance. Music is often used during these festivals to keep up the pace of the event, or for cultural reasons for example they believe it'll keep bad luck away from your life
