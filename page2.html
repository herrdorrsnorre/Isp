Climate

Hong Kong climate is considered subtropical however it's proximity to the ocean gives it a temperate climate during the winter that averages 14 degrees celsius with humid summers that averages 30 degrees celsius

On average hong kong receives 30 cyclones per year 6 of which become typhoons with and average 94 inches of rainfall
